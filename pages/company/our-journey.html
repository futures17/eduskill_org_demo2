OUR MILESTONES

Our Journey

From a small foundational team to a global AI-EdTech talent group. Follow the timeline of our milestones and integrations.

2011–12

Foundation

EduSkill Technologies Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated with the core objective of bridging skills gaps in standard academic graduates.

2013

International Partnerships

Established key strategic collaborations with European technology institutions and academic councils to standardize frameworks.

2014

Placement Division

Formed the dedicated Corporate Placement Cell connecting partner colleges directly to retail and tech recruiters.

2015

K12 Expansion

Introduced standardized vocational and math skilling frameworks into school systems across northern states in India.

2016

Pan India Presence

Successfully scaled operations into major cities, opening offices in Prayagraj, Noida, and Mumbai hubs.

2017

Mega Job Fairs

Initiated first mega job fairs, hosting 100+ recruiters and onboarding thousands of retail career aspirants in single drives.

2018

Sector Specific Training

Launched customized skilling tracks aligned with NSDC mandates for retail, hospitality, and IT service desks.

2019

MOOC + Udacity Partnerships

Integrated global learning materials and online courses, offering standardized pathways to micro-credentials.

2020

Global Employability Partnerships

Formed partnerships in Singapore and Switzerland, transitioning into an AI-driven global talent ecosystem.

EduSkill Group is a unified, AI-powered EdTech ecosystem connecting specialist platforms to empower students, institutions, and employers.
